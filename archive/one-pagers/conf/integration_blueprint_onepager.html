Control Layers
Layer 1 — Authenticity
Is the document genuine?
Physics-level forensic signals: FFT, PRNU, ELA, moiré, double-compression. Generator-agnostic — works on models released today and tomorrow.
DocLens · FRC L1/L2/L3 codes
Metrics: BR · FPR · GS
Layer 2 — Agent Provenance
Is submission context trustworthy?
Agent attestation, delegation chain integrity, tool call attribution, connector allowlist compliance, HITL assertion presence.
FRC A2A · L0/L0-D codes
Metrics: ABR · CDR · TCR · HAR
Layer 3 — Decision Traceability
Can we reproduce and audit this?
Structured reason codes per signal. instrumentation_trace for every tool call. Reproducible audit record for regulators, QA, and model governance.
FRC schema · audit_trail
Metrics: TCR · HAR · escalation SLA
Compound Verdict Outcomes
TRUSTED
